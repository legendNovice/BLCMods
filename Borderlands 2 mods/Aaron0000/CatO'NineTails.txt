    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Turns the Scorpio into a blaster interpretation of the Pre-Sequel Cat o' Nine Tails.

        Features;

        Divides into nine ricochetting projectiles upon hitting a surface.

        Reduced fire rate.

        Only one ammo consumed per shot instead of two.

        Spawns in all elements instead of just fire (but not non-elemental).

        Note; Uses the firing mode of the Emperor to achieve the effects (but alters it to use the default smg firing mode). Beware if using mods that affect that firing mode.
